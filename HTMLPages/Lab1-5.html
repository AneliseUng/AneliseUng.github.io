Almond Green Salad

Fresh and crispy

ingredients

1/2 pound bacon
2 tablespoons vinegar
3 tablespoons honey
1/2 teaspoon mustard
1/2 teaspoon celery salt
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1 head red leaf lettuce, bite sized pieces
1 can of mandarin oranges
3/4 cup slivered almonds

Directions

Cook bacon, draian, cool.

Blend vinegar, honey, dry mustard.

Place lettuce, oranges, green onions, bacon, almonds, toss w/ dressing and serve.
