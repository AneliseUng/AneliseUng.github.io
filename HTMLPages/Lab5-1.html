Works of Vincent Van Gogh

The Starry Night

Vincent VAn Gogh painted staryy night in 1889 during his stay at the asylum of saint-paul-de-mausole near saint-remy-de-province. Van gogh livd well in the hospital; he was allowed more freedoms than any of the other patients. If attended, he could leave the hospital grounds; he was allowed to paint, read, write, adn withdraw nto his own room.

Sunflowers

Van Gogh's paintings of Sunflowers are among his most famous. He did them in Aries, in the south of France, in 1888 and 1889. Vincent painted a total of five large canvases with sunflowers in a vase, with three shades of yellow 'and nothing else'.
